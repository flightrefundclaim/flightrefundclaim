Terms & Conditions

Last updated: January 2025

Our Service

FlightRefundClaim provides a flight compensation claims service on behalf of air passengers under EC Regulation 261/2004 and UK261. We are not a law firm and do not provide legal advice.

No Win, No Fee

Our service is provided on a no win, no fee basis. You will not be charged any fee unless we successfully recover compensation on your behalf. Upon successful recovery, our fee is 30% (inclusive of VAT where applicable) of the total compensation amount received.

Your Authority

By submitting your claim and signing the Letter of Authority, you authorise FlightRefundClaim to act as your representative in pursuing compensation. This includes submitting claim letters, corresponding with the airline, and escalating to regulatory authorities on your behalf.

Your Obligations

  • Provide accurate and complete information about your flight
  • Notify us of any direct contact from the airline regarding your claim
  • Not pursue the same claim directly or through another party simultaneously
  • Provide any additional documents we request in a timely manner

Eligibility

Submitting a claim does not guarantee compensation. Eligibility is determined by EC261/2004 and UK261 regulations. We reserve the right to decline claims we do not consider viable.

Electronic Signature

Your typed name in the Letter of Authority constitutes a valid electronic signature under the UK Electronic Communications Act 2000 and EU eIDAS Regulation, and is legally binding.

Time Limits

Claims must be submitted within the legal time limits — 6 years in England and Wales, varying by jurisdiction elsewhere.

Governing Law

These Terms are governed by the laws of England and Wales. Any disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the English courts.
