# FlightRefundClaim Tracking Link Playbook

Use the links in `tracked-campaign-links.csv` whenever you promote the site. This lets Analytics show which channel produced visits, WhatsApp clicks, form contacts and claim submissions.

## First actions

1. Put the Facebook profile link in your page/profile bio.
2. Use the general post links for normal Facebook posts.
3. Use the WhatsApp links when sending the site manually.
4. Add the email signature link to outgoing business emails.
5. Use community links only when your comment genuinely answers the question.

## Copy

UK:

Delayed or cancelled flight from the UK or EU? You may be able to claim up to £520 or €600 per passenger. Free eligibility check, no win no fee.

US:

Flying home from Europe and your flight was delayed or cancelled? US passengers can still be protected by UK and EU flight compensation rules. Free eligibility check, no win no fee.

Community reply:

If your flight departed from the UK or EU and arrived more than 3 hours late, it may be worth checking eligibility. The rules depend on route, airline, delay length and the reason the airline gave.
