About FlightRefundClaim

FlightRefundClaim helps US travellers affected by UK or EU flights recover compensation for delayed, cancelled and overbooked flights under EC261/2004 and UK261.

Check Your Claim Free

No Win, No Fee

Our fee is 30% of recovered compensation and is charged only after a successful recovery.

Passenger-Rights Focus

We review each claim honestly, prepare the airline submission, handle follow-up correspondence and escalate eligible cases where appropriate.

Clear Scope

We are a passenger-rights claims service, not a law firm. We focus on US travellers when their route is protected by UK or EU passenger-rights rules.

How We Help

FlightRefundClaim reviews flight disruption claims, checks whether the route and circumstances may be covered, prepares claim correspondence and supports passengers through airline follow-up. If we do not recover compensation for you, you do not pay us a service fee.

Fees And Compensation

Eligible claims can be worth up to about $650 per passenger depending on distance, delay length, cancellation notice and airline responsibility. Our success fee is 30% of recovered compensation.
