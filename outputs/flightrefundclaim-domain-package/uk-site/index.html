Process

How It Works

Three simple steps. We handle the heavy lifting while you wait for your compensation.

01

Check Your Eligibility

Answer 8 simple questions about your flight. Takes under 2 minutes. We'll tell you immediately if you have a valid claim and exactly how much you're owed.

02

We Fight For You

Our team prepares your formal claim letter under EC261/2004 or UK261 and submits it to the airline. If they reject — we escalate to the Civil Aviation Authority.

03

You Get Paid

When we win, we transfer your compensation minus our 30% fee via bank transfer, PayPal or Wise. If we don't win, you pay absolutely nothing. Guaranteed.
