What is EU261?

EU261 is the passenger rights law that sets fixed compensation for eligible disruption. It can apply regardless of your nationality when the flight itself is covered.

Why use FlightRefundClaim?

Airlines often reject valid claims or ask for repeated evidence. We prepare the claim, handle correspondence and escalate where appropriate.

FAQ

Common Questions

Who is covered by EU261?

Passengers departing from an EU airport are usually covered, and some flights into the EU on EU airlines are also covered.

How much is EU261 compensation?

Compensation is commonly EUR250, EUR400 or EUR600 per passenger depending on flight distance.

Is EU261 only for EU citizens?

No. Coverage is based mainly on the flight route and airline, not the passenger’s nationality.
